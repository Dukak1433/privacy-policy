Privacy Policy

QR & Barcode Scanner

Last Updated: June 30, 2026  |  Developer: Kodivra Labs

📋 Summary

This app works entirely offline for its core features. Your scan history stays on your device and is never sent to our servers. The app may show ads and offer optional premium features to support free access.

1. Camera Permission

Camera access is used solely to scan QR codes and barcodes. No images or video are stored, transmitted, or shared with any server.

2. Storage Permission

Storage access may be used to save generated QR images, export scan history, and read gallery images for scanning. All files remain on your device and are never uploaded anywhere.

3. Local Database

Scan history, labels, and favorites are stored in a local SQLite database on your device. This data never leaves your device.

4. Wi-Fi Permission

Used only to connect to a network when a Wi-Fi QR code is scanned. No network data is collected or transmitted.

5. Services We Use

The app works entirely offline. We only integrate with the following Google services:

  • Google AdMob (Advertising): To support free usage, Google's Advertising ID may be anonymously processed to display relevant ads.
  • Google Play Billing (Premium): Premium feature purchases are handled securely through Google Play. We do not store any payment information.

6. Third-Party Services

The app may use the following Google services:

  • Google AdMob — for displaying ads
  • Google Play Billing — for premium feature purchases
  • Google Play Services — for app verification and updates

7. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
