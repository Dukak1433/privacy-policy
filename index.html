Privacy Policy

QR & Barcode Scanner

Effective Date: June 23, 2026  |  Developer: Kodivra Labs

🔒 Summary

QR & Barcode Scanner does not collect, store, or share any personal data with third parties. All data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely on your device (offline-first). No data is sent to any server.

2. Camera Permission

The app requests access to your device camera solely for scanning QR codes and barcodes. No images or video are stored, transmitted, or shared.

3. Storage Permission

The app may request storage access to save generated QR images and export scan history. All files remain on your device.

4. Local Database

Scan history is stored in a local SQLite database on your device. This data never leaves your device.

5. Wi-Fi Permission

Used only to connect to Wi-Fi when a Wi-Fi QR code is scanned. No network data is collected.

6. Third-Party Services

The app does not include any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs.

7. Children's Privacy

The app does not collect any information from children under 13.
