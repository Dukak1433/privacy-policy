Privacy Policy

QR & Barcode Scanner | Last Updated: June 30, 2026 | Developer: Kodivra Labs
📋 Summary: This app works entirely offline. Your scan history stays on your device and is never sent to any server. No ads, no analytics, no data collection.

1. Camera Permission

Camera access is used solely to scan QR codes and barcodes. No images or video are stored, transmitted, or shared.

2. Storage Permission

Storage access may be used to save generated QR images, export scan history, and read gallery images for scanning. All files remain on your device.

3. Local Database

Scan history, labels, and favorites are stored in a local SQLite database on your device. This data never leaves your device.

4. Wi-Fi Permission

Used only to connect to a network when a Wi-Fi QR code is scanned. No network data is collected or transmitted.

5. Third-Party Services

This app does not include any analytics, advertising, or crash reporting SDKs. No data is shared with third parties.

6. Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
